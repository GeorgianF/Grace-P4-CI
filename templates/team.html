Meet the team

Executive Chef

Curtis Duffy

The head chef Curtis Duffy and his business partner Michael Muser opened Grace in December 206. Duffy had previously worked at Charlie Trotter's and was chef de cuisine at Alinea when it opened in 2004. In the years prior to opening Grace, he maintained a two star Michelin rating while the head chef at Avenues in the Peninsula Hotel until closing the restaurant in 2011.

Grace has maintained a Michelin Guide three-star restaurant status since 2014 and to date is only the third restaurant in Chicago ever to earn three Michelin stars after Alinea

Sommelier

Richard Wijnhaus

Richard has been involved with Grace from the very first moment. The innovative dishes of the kitchen team are complimented with verve by the wine choices of Wine Director Richard Wijnhaus.

He takes his profession very seriously. We are not the only ones who appreciate his knowledge and craftsmanship. Richard has been voted best sommelier for the years 2018-2019 and plays this role with verve and passion in Grace. Always in a good mood and with excellent wine-food combinations.
